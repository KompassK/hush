How to play

Swap two side-by-side stones to line up three or more of the same colour and shape. They pop, and new stones drift down to fill the gaps.

Line up five or more, or make a 2×2 square, for a little extra pop. The board keeps flowing on its own.

There's no score, and no winning or losing. Play for as long as you like.

The gear lets you choose how the pops look. The clock just shows your play time.

Rest well.
